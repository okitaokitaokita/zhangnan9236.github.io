Projects

Jan 2026 – May 2026 Group Project (MSc)

Smart City Surveillance Video Analysis System

Intelligent monitoring system to analyze urban surveillance videos — detecting abnormal events and providing traffic statistics to improve city safety and efficiency.

Data

VIRAT public dataset — multiple outdoor scenes with annotated pedestrian and vehicle activities.

Approach

Background subtraction with multi-period background models for low-light conditions; integrated optical flow (TV-L1, CLG) and YOLO for motion estimation and real-time object detection; designed event detection logic for anomaly recognition.

Outcome

Detection accuracy mAP@0.5 > 0.85; reduced false positives; generated hourly traffic flow curves and group size histograms, enhancing interpretability for city planners.

My Contribution

Video preprocessing, background modeling, motion analysis, traffic statistics visualization, and validation of event detection.

Dec 2022 – Jun 2023 Individual (BSc Thesis)

Stock Price Prediction using LSTM

Deep learning model to predict future stock prices from historical data, addressing non-linear temporal relationships in financial time series.

Data

Historical daily stock price data (open, high, low, close, volume) from Yahoo Finance — 5+ years of data.

Approach

Built an LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) neural network; performed data normalization, sequence generation, and hyperparameter tuning; evaluated using RMSE and MAE.

Outcome

LSTM outperformed baseline linear models — RMSE significantly lower than ARIMA; demonstrated model stability via cross-validation.

My Contribution

Independently completed the entire pipeline — data collection, preprocessing, model design, training, evaluation, and result interpretation.
